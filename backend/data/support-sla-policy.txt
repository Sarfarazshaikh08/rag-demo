Priority 1 support issues must receive first response within 15 minutes.

Priority 1 support issues must have a workaround or resolution plan within 4 hours.

Priority 2 support issues must receive first response within 1 business hour.

Priority 2 support issues must have a workaround or resolution plan within 1 business day.

Priority 3 support issues must receive first response within 4 business hours.

Priority 3 support issues must be resolved or scheduled within 3 business days.

Support tickets must include client name, affected system, business impact, screenshots or logs, priority, and requested outcome.

Production incidents must be documented with timeline, impact, root cause, corrective action, and prevention action.

Any repeated incident within 30 days must be reviewed by support operations and delivery engineering.

Client communication during Priority 1 incidents must be updated every 60 minutes until a workaround is confirmed.

SLA breach risk must be escalated to the support manager before the SLA timer expires.
