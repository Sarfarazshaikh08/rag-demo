Employees must treat colleagues, clients, vendors, and partners with respect and professionalism.

Harassment, discrimination, intimidation, and retaliation are strictly prohibited.

Conflicts of interest must be disclosed to the reporting manager and legal team.

Gifts from vendors above 2,000 INR must be reported to the compliance team.

Employees must not accept cash gifts from vendors, clients, or partners.

Company confidential information must not be discussed in public areas or shared with unauthorized parties.

Social media posts must not disclose confidential company information or client information.

Employees must follow all applicable laws, regulations, and company compliance requirements.

Suspected misconduct can be reported to HR, Legal, or the ethics helpline.

Retaliation against anyone who reports a concern in good faith is prohibited.

Violations of the code of conduct may result in disciplinary action up to termination.
