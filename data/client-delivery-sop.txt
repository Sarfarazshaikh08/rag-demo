Client onboarding must begin only after the signed proposal, NDA, purchase order, and billing contact details are received.

The delivery kickoff call must be scheduled within 2 business days after commercial confirmation.

The project manager must publish the kickoff agenda, stakeholder list, communication plan, and milestone plan before the kickoff call.

Every client delivery project must have a named executive sponsor, project manager, technical owner, and client success owner.

Weekly status reports must include milestone progress, open risks, blockers, decisions needed, and next-week priorities.

Client decisions that affect scope, budget, or timeline must be captured in writing before delivery work continues.

Change requests must include business reason, estimated effort, timeline impact, and commercial impact before approval.

Delivery teams must update the project tracker every Friday before 5:00 PM IST.

Client acceptance requires sign-off on scope completion, test evidence, pending issues, and handover notes.

Post-go-live hypercare runs for 10 business days unless the statement of work defines a different support window.

Project closure requires final documentation, credential handover, knowledge transfer, and finance confirmation.
