Password reset policy: Employees must reset passwords every 90 days.

Passwords must contain at least 12 characters with uppercase letters, lowercase letters, numbers, and symbols.

Multi-factor authentication is mandatory for email, VPN, HRMS, finance systems, and production systems.

Company laptops must use disk encryption and approved endpoint protection.

USB storage devices are not allowed unless approved by the IT security team.

Employees must report suspected phishing emails to the security team within 2 hours.

Lost or stolen devices must be reported to IT and the reporting manager immediately.

Production access is granted only after manager approval and security review.

Shared accounts are prohibited for business systems and production environments.

Confidential data must not be stored on personal cloud drives or personal devices.

VPN must be used when accessing internal systems outside the office network.

Security incidents are reviewed by IT, Legal, and the relevant business owner.
