Paid leave entitlement: Employees are entitled to 12 paid leaves per calendar year.

Casual leave can be taken with prior approval from the reporting manager.

Sick leave requires medical proof if the leave exceeds 2 consecutive days.

Remote work: Employees are allowed to work remotely up to 2 days per week with manager approval.

Office working hours are from 9:30 AM to 6:30 PM, Monday to Friday.

Late login beyond 10:00 AM will be considered half-day if repeated frequently.

Employees must inform their manager at least 1 day in advance before taking planned leave.

Emergency leave can be taken without prior approval but must be informed within the same day.

Company provides 10 public holidays each year as per the official holiday calendar.

Employees are required to complete a minimum of 40 working hours per week.

Performance reviews are conducted twice a year.

Employees are eligible for a salary revision based on performance during annual review.

Confidential company information must not be shared outside the organization.

Use of company devices is restricted to official purposes only.

Any violation of company policies may result in disciplinary action.

Probation period for new employees is 90 days unless the offer letter states otherwise.

Employees must complete mandatory compliance training within 30 days of joining.

Payroll inputs must be finalized by the 24th day of each month.

Resignation notice period is 30 days for employees below manager level and 60 days for manager level and above.

Exit clearance must be completed with HR, IT, Finance, and the reporting manager before the final settlement is processed.
