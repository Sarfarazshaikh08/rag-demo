Expense reimbursement claims must be submitted within 30 days of the expense date.

Original receipts are mandatory for all expense claims above 500 INR.

Meal reimbursement during business travel is capped at 1,200 INR per day.

Domestic travel bookings must be approved by the reporting manager before tickets are purchased.

International travel requires approval from the department head and finance team.

Cab reimbursement is allowed for official travel between office, client location, airport, railway station, and hotel.

Personal expenses, alcohol, tobacco, and entertainment expenses are not reimbursable.

Hotel reimbursement must follow the city-wise limits published by the finance team.

Expense claims are processed every Friday after finance verification.

Advance payments must be settled within 7 working days after travel completion.

Policy violations may result in rejection of the claim and escalation to the reporting manager.
