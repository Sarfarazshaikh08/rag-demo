A project must be escalated when a milestone is at risk by more than 3 business days.

A project must be escalated when scope changes are requested without written commercial approval.

A project must be escalated when a client blocker remains unresolved for more than 2 business days.

Severity 1 delivery escalations must be reported to the project manager, delivery head, and client sponsor within 1 hour.

Severity 2 delivery escalations must be reported to the project manager and delivery head within 4 business hours.

Severity 3 delivery escalations must be tracked in the weekly status report with owner and due date.

Budget overrun risk above 10 percent must be escalated to finance and the business head.

Resource unavailability for more than 1 business day on a critical path task must be escalated to the delivery head.

Client dissatisfaction or negative executive feedback must be escalated to client success within the same business day.

Escalation closure requires root cause, corrective action, owner, due date, and client communication summary.
